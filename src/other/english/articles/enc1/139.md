## Is that you, John? 8

- 1.Is that you, John?

- 2.Yes, speaking


- 3.Tell Mary we'll be late for dinner this evening.

- 4.I'm afraid I don't understand.


- 5.Hasn't Mary told you?

- 6.She invited Charlotte and me to dinner this evening.

- 7.I said I would be at your house at six o'clock, but the boss wants me to do some extra work.

- 8.I'll have to stay at the office.

- 9.I don't know when I'll finish.

- 10.Oh, and by the way, my wife wants to know if Mary needs any help.

- 11.I don't know what you're talking about.


- 12.That is John Smith, isn't it?

- 13.Yes, I'm John Smith.


- 14.You are John Smith, the engineer, aren't you?

- 15.That's right.
  

- 16.You work for the Overseas Engineering Company, don't you?

- 17.No, I don't.

- 18.I'm John Smith the telephone engineer and I'm repairing  `/rɪˈper/`  your telephone line.
